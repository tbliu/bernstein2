What is Bernstein?

Bernstein lets you discover if your politican is being influenced by dark money, as well as the likely political positions of the anonymous groups funding him or her. Enter a candidate's name in the search bar at the top right corner to get started.

What is dark money?

Dark money is one of the consequences of the controversial Supreme Court case FEC v. Citizens United. It refers to potentially unlimited funds that are given by organizations and corporations to politicians. In addition, these corporations and organizations are allowed to remain anonymous.

Why is it a problem?

The unlimited amounts of dark money that politicans and organizations receive can be easily used to influence elections. In addition, many believe that dark money is a violation of free speech. Those who have more money and are able to donate large amounts anonymously have more say in politics than those who don't.

About Bernstein

Bernstein works by comparing politicians' voting records with the positions of their known donors. We assume that a politican's voting record should correspond perfectly with the position of his or her known donors. However, if there is some disparity, then we know that there is dark money in play.
